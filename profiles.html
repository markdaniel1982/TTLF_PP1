Jonni Bryant

He discovered 'Hard House' not by mistake but by an opportunity presenting itself to him and the universe gracing him with the sound that ultimately would never leave his side. From the age of 16, Jonni, had purchased his first pair of turntables. He had discovered pioneers within the industry that set him off on a path of self-discovery. At the age of 17 he experienced great loss through suicide and cancer, and with this loss he leaned on 'Hard House' to move forward with his life, and it then began to create opportunities within his adolescent life, that would change his life forever. With events such as Sundissential, Polysexual, Godskitchen, Insomniacz and Progress from 2000, this propelled him forward to wanting to influence future generations through having these experiences. This led him onto attaining regular residency spots at events across the UK and Europe. The Passion and drive instilled through this musical influence in his life has led him to starting his own brand, and further his production skills. The vision is simple, to share my passion and love for electronic dance music to bring people together, and to create a unique sound and long-lasting memories.

Josh B

Hailing from Coventry, DJ Josh B is our newest member of the TTLF Family. Josh B purchased his first set of decks back in the year 2000 aged just 17! After 1 year of being a bedroom DJ, he secured his first weekly residency at Coventry's first hard house after club, the legendary 'FIDGET'. From there Josh soon began his own monthly night called 'Satisfaction' which ran up until 2003. Josh then decided it was time to take his DJ skills to the wider international audience and secured a residency in 'STAR BEACH', Malia Greece, before moving to Leeds in the same year. Josh currently lives back in Coventry and up until recently has been on pirate radio stations all across the Midlands but is hungry to bring back the days when he played at huge 'hard house' party's such as Sundissential North, B2t, Fidget and Crazy Daisy's to name but a few! Josh B prides himself on his friendly but high energy performances on the decks whilst always playing 1 or 2 rare tunes that you may not of heard before! We are proud to have him as our newest resident DJ, at TTLF!

Chris James

Chris fell upon the harder sound in around the year 2001 when commercial trance style tunes just didn't cut it! Sundissential then became the norm...and it defined him! In 2002 he began dj'ing and whilst serving away mastered his craft. In 2004 he started to poke into the Birmingham based circuit and featured in afters parties such as Shaft & Shine. The most notable and probably greatest achievement was playing alongside artists such as 12 inch thumpers, Ian M, Rafferty & Kershaw for Sundissential vs Afters! The years that followed were littered with private and personal gigs in Germany mainly, but the love for the sound never faltered! Now with a wisdom of understanding and a desire to keep the flame burning for our sound, Chris hopes to re-ignite the musical memory paths that you have embedded in your brain, to bring smiles and good times back to life.
